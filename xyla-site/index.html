01 · The Problem

Supply Chains Are the Kill Zone

Operational endurance is determined by logistics. In recent conflicts, supply infrastructure has proven more vulnerable than frontline units. Low-cost autonomous systems can disable high-value logistics vehicles, shifting the advantage toward those who can operate without continuous resupply.


Forward units go combat-ineffective within 72 hours of resupply cutoff. The same constraint applies beyond the battlefield: research stations, disaster zones, maritime deployments, and long-duration space missions. The environment may change, but the dependency doesn't.
